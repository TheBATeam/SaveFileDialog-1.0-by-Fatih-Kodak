
Savefiledialog - Open a dialog to save a file..

Call:  savefiledialog.exe [-h|-v] <title> <default file> <pattern> <pos>

Options:
          -h        Print this message
          -v        Print version number

Examples: savefiledialog.exe "Save as" Default.txt "All Files (*.*)|*.*"
          savefiledialog.exe "Save As" "" "Script (*.js;*.vbs)|*.js;*.vbs"
          savefiledialog.exe "Save As" "" "*.* (*.*)|*.*|BAT (*.bat)|*.bat" 1
